How to get the beautiful probability density function (pdf) of Normal Distribution

2020-08-13

How can we derive the probability density function (pdf) of Normal Distribution? \[f_Y(y)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma}e^{-\frac{1}{2}(\frac{y-\mu}{\sigma})^2}, \quad -\infty<y<+\infty\]

           xlab="x" , ylab="p(x)" , cex.lab=1.5,

text( 0, .5*max(y) , bquote( paste(sigma ," = 1 ") )
      , cex=1.5 )

Let \(W_1\), \(W_2\), . . . be a set of random variables with mean \(\mu\) and variance \(\sigma^2\), and their moment-generating functions are \(M_{W_i}(t), \quad i=1,2,...,n\) Let \(S_i=(W_i-\mu)/\sigma\), then \(E(S_i)=0\) and \(Var(S_i)=1\).
